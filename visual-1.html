Max Temperature


As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 degree latitude). However, the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of year when data was gathered. Temperatures drop the further an area is from the equator due to the curvature of the earth.
